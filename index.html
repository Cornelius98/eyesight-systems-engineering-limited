About The Company

Eyesight Engineering Limited is a proudly Zambian owned company specializing in maintenance, installation and supply of electrical, instrumentation, ICT products and engineering support services.

Established in 2025 and headquartered in Chibombo, Central Province, our firm was created to meet the growing demand for quality construction and engineering services in Zambia and the surrounding regions.

We offer a diverse range of services that blend technical excellence with innovation, ensuring that our clients receive long-lasting and cost-effective solutions. Our strength lies in our local understanding of the Zambian infrastructure landscape, our experienced team, and our commitment to community-driven development.
